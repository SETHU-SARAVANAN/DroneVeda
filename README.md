# DroneVeda

> **Intelligence in Every Flight.**
> 
> Commercial UAV systems, custom industrial drones, autonomous agriculture, and institutional robotics training.

## Overview

**DroneVeda** operates at the intersection of advanced aerial robotics and aerospace education. We engineer end-to-end bespoke UAV systems for specialized industries, ranging from heavy-lift agricultural sprayers to LiDAR-equipped industrial mapping drones. Beyond manufacturing, DroneVeda is dedicated to shaping the next generation of engineers through structured, hands-on drone building and programming workshops.

## Key Divisions

### Innovation
Real projects moving beyond the classroom and into the field:
* **Crop Monitoring Drone**: Solar-powered drones for real-time crop health monitoring using multispectral sensors and AI-assisted analysis.
* **Industrial Inspection**: Custom UAVs built for infrastructure inspection with thermal imaging, LiDAR payloads, and autonomous waypoint navigation.
* **Robotics & Bots**: Custom robotics projects for academic competitions, including line followers, maze solvers, and sensor-driven autonomous bots.
* **Educational Kits**: Complete hands-on workshop kits for schools covering drone assembly, programming modules, and guided flight sessions.

### Training (Workshops)
Structured programs for aspiring drone engineers—from first principles to real-world autonomous systems:
* **Beginner (Build & Fly)**: Full quadcopter assembly and real-time flight control practice.
* **Intermediate (Tune & Control)**: Flight controller calibration, ESC tuning, and basic autonomous logic.
* **Advanced (Autonomous UAVs)**: GPS integration, mission planning, and industry-grade flight controllers.

### Manufacturing (Custom Builds)
End-to-end design and manufacturing of bespoke UAV systems:
* **Agricultural Drones**: Heavy-lift quadcopters and hexacopters engineered for precision crop spraying and multispectral imaging.
* **RC Agri Rovers**: Remote-controlled ground rovers designed to navigate complex terrain and conduct soil analysis.
* **Industrial UAVs**: LiDAR and thermal-equipped drones for construction site mapping and search-and-rescue operations.

## Our Process

Every custom build starts with understanding the operational environment, payload requirements, and mission profile. We prototype, test, and iterate until it flies exactly the way it needs to.
1. **Discovery Call**: Understanding environment, payload, and mission requirements.
2. **Design & Prototype**: CAD modeling, component selection, and rapid prototype builds.
3. **Field Testing**: Controlled test flights with live data logging and iteration.
4. **Delivery & Support**: Handover with training, documentation, and ongoing support.
